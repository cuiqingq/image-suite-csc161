pixelStrip: The image R G or B component increased depending
on the option chosen as expected and shown by the output picture.

pictureRedder: The output picture's red component was greater and the other
two decreased as expected.

pictureBluer:The output picture's blue component was greater and the other
two decreased as expected.

pictureGreener: The output picture's green component was greater and the other
two decreased as expected.

circleSelect: The pixels inside the circle specified are adjusted to
grayscale values as expected.

pictureEdges: Generates a new picture that has pixel values reflecting the
change in brightness of the original picture.
